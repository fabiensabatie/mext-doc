# **Partners & Exhibitors Feedback**

Partners and exhibitors have the option to share their feedback and questions with you directly through their interface. When you click on the** Exhibitor's Feedback button**, you gain access to their feedback. 

 ![](/img/Marvaimages/Documentationpartnersfeedbacka.jpg)

Additionally, by clicking the **eye icon**, you can view the** specific details** of the **exhibitor's feedback**, and  you can respond to them by email.

 ![](/img/Marvaimages/documentationfeedbackreply.jpg)

and you can **respond** to them by **email.**
