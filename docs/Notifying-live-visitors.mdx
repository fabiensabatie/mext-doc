# **Notifying live visitors**

You have the capability to broadcast messages to all attendees and live visitors in your 3D immersive space and metaverse, whether it's an announcement, important information, or advertisements, using the “Notifying Live Visitors” feature in the administrator Backoffice .

 

This message will immediately appear on the attendees' screens and will also be posted in the chat for their convenience.
