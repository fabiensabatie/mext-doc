# Create/edit your events

The Mext platform features an advanced event software that offers seamless integration. In this section, you will discover how to effectively create, manage, and promote your events. With the comprehensive event management capabilities provided by the platform, you can streamline the entire event lifecycle, from planning to execution and promotion.

![Mext metaverse Dashboard Features](/img/metaverse/create-your-events-add.jpg)

* **Title**: Name your event
* **Short description**: This will be used on pages that list your event.
* **Long description**: This will be displayed on your event's page.
* **Start date / End date**: Set the duration of your event.
* **Square image**: This will be displayed on the Mext website explore page.
* **Banner image**: This will be displayed on your event's page.
* **Flyer**: Provide more information abour your event. This will be displayed on your event's page.
* **Hall by default**: Where users will land first. Only applies to metaverses that have mulmtiple scenes (Professional / Custom plans only)
* **Enable apply as exhibitor**: By selecting this option, you allow companies to submit requests to showcase their products and services in their dedicated 3D space (e.g., as an exhibitor). You can add Terms and Conditions for exhibitors in the General Information section, and if necessary, specify pricing offers in the ticketing section.
* **Enable apply as visitor**: People can apply to access your event as a public visitor. This allows you to collect more information about the audience.
* **Enable call for speakers**: Choosing this option lets individuals express their interest in speaking during the conference.
* **Speakers**: Include speakers who have already registered, gradually populating the event page with their information. You can add Terms and Conditions for speakers in the General Information section and define pricing offers in the ticketing section if required.
* **Sponsors**: Incorporate sponsors who have already registered, allowing you to prominently feature them on the event page.

> To maximize the visibility and reach of your event, consider the following suggestions\:Create an attractive banner and description: Craft a visually appealing banner and compelling description for your event, incorporating relevant keywords. This will not only enhance the aesthetics but also improve the event's search engine optimization (SEO) to increase its discoverability.
> Promote your event on social media: Extend the promotion of your event by creating similar event pages on various social media platforms. Utilize the same banner and description to maintain consistency and brand recognition. Be sure to include the link to your event page, directing interested participants to the main source of information. By implementing these recommendations, you can boost the visibility and engagement of your event, attracting a wider audience and generating greater interest in your Metaverse activities or events.
