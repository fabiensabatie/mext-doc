## How can I add a new channel for streaming?

* Click on “Create another channel” 

![](/img/Documentationspeedchannel.jpg)

To create a channel, head to **Livestreaming sessions & broadcasting** on your [dashboard](https://dashboard.mext.app/metaverse), then click on **Add new channel**.
You can [learn more about channels here](/docs/metaverse/manage-your-events/metaverse-tv/how-channels-work).

* **Name**: Specify the name of your channel.
* **Source URL**: You can either click on **Generate Channel** to automatically create the URL, or use your own if you already have one.
* **Description**: Include a description of channel to be displayed in the metaverse.
* **Broadcast URL**: You can either click on **Generate Channel** to automatically create the URL, or use your own if you already have one.
* **Broadcast Stream Key**: You can either click on **Generate Channel** to automatically create the stream key, or use your own if you already have one.

Then click on **Confirm** to create the channel.

![Mext metaverse Dashboard Features Add Channel](/img/metaverse/add-channel.jpg)

## Using the channel

You can now either :

* Head to [the metaverse editor](/docs/metaverse/manage-your-metaverse/customize-your-metaverse/metaverse-editor) to [insert a video player](/docs/metaverse/manage-your-metaverse/customize-your-metaverse/types-of-models/videos-and-screens), and configure it to use your channel.
* [Create a meeting](/docs/metaverse/manage-your-events/video-meetings/create-a-meeting) (Zoom call), and [broadcast it to your channel](/docs/metaverse/manage-your-events/metaverse-tv/broadcasting-a-meeting)
