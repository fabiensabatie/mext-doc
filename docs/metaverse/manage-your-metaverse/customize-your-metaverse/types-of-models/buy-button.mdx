# Buy button

This features allows you to redirect users to your online e-commerce shop. You can [upload your own 3D model of your article](/docs/metaverse/manage-your-metaverse/customize-your-metaverse/metaverse-editor#adding-a-model), then add a **Buy button** to supercharge your sales. You can change the button's style and use your own image instead.

To get started, click on the **<span style={{color: "green", fontSize: "40px"}}>+</span>** button located in the toolbar on the left side of the builder. In the new window, search for **Buy button**.
