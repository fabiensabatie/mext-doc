To get started, click on the **<span style={{color: "green", fontSize: "40px"}}>+</span>** button located in the toolbar on the left side of the builder. In the new window, search for **Custom Video**.

![Mext metaverse Dashboard Features Metaverse Editor Panel](/img/metaverse/custom-video.png)

## Types of content available

Video screens allow you to display either a **Mext channel**, a **Twitch channel**, a **YouTube video**, or a **custom video file**.
To a edit the content of a screen, click on it in [the builder](/docs/metaverse/manage-your-metaverse/customize-your-metaverse/metaverse-editor), then under **Panel content** of the [properties editor](/docs/metaverse/manage-your-metaverse/customize-your-metaverse/types-of-models/videos-and-screens#video-properties), select the type of content you wish to display.

## Frequent use cases

### Streaming or broadcasting a meeting

On Mext, we have a meetings & gathering feature that allows you to create meetings, invite people, and share it live to your audience. Head to the [related section in the documentation](/docs/metaverse/manage-your-events/metaverse-tv/broadcasting-a-meeting)

### Streaming content live from a computer

There is plenty of existing software that allows you to stream content from your computer to your metaverse. To learn more about them and how to connect them to Mext, check out [how channels works](/docs/metaverse/manage-your-events/metaverse-tv/how-channels-work)
