# Photos

To get started, click on the **<span style={{color: "green", fontSize: "40px"}}>+</span>** button located in the toolbar on the left side of the builder. In the new window, search for **Custom Picture**.

To upload your own picture, click on the element in the 3D view, then select your file in the **Panel content** of the [properties editor](/docs/metaverse/manage-your-metaverse/customize-your-metaverse/types-of-models/photos#photo-properties).
