The landing page tool allows you to create a customized landing web page for your 3D interactive space and metaverse. It simplifies the process with no-code requirements, enabling you to effortlessly embed it into your website or any other platform. Additionally, you can make it visible through the Mext website for easy access.

### To create the landing page, make sur to add the below information, 

* Write the name of your 3D interactive space and metaverse,
* Make it private or public depending on whether your platform is for public (everyone can access) or private (people
  can access only through a link that you need to send it to them). You can also make it private when it is on progress and not ready to be seen, 
* Add your Metaverse description, 
* Add “logo” and save. The logo will be used into all the transactional emails,
* Add “Cover photo” to be seen on website and save.

### **Enhance your Metaverse experience with these features:**

* PDF Flyer Upload: Effortlessly incorporate a PDF flyer onto your Landing page to provide additional information and offer visitors the chance to download a document detailing your 3D interactive space and Metaverse
* Customized T\&C (Terms & Conditions of use ): Tailor the terms of use for your 3D interactive space and Metaverse.
  Visitors can review and agree to these terms before accessing your Metaverse.
* Visibility on Mext website: While your 3D interactive space and Metaverse is automatically visible on the Mext website, you have the flexibility to disable this function.
* Partner/Exhibitor Application: Enable a button for visitors to apply as partners, creating dedicated spaces within your 3D interactive spaces and Metaverse. Whether it's an exhibitor in your Metaverse exhibition or a shop owner in your Metaverse Mall, you have the power to grant them access and to moderate the space of the
  partner.
* Private Metaverse Option: If you choose a private Metaverse setting, visitors must apply for access, ensuring a controlled and exclusive experience tailored to your selected audience.
