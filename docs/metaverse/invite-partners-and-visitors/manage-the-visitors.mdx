You have access to the complete list of your registered visitors, and here are the various actions you can take:

 

•	You can search for visitors by their name, email, and other criteria.

•	You have the option to filter visitors by the event they are associated with.

•	Visitors can be filtered based on their sources:

o	"Admin" signifies visitors added by an administrator.

o	"Website" represents visitors who registered via the website to gain entry.

o	"Join Metaverse" designates visitors who joined without registration because they are already registered at the Mext Platform.

•	You can download your visitor list, which proves beneficial when you want to incorporate the list into your own Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system.

•	It is possible to upload a list of visitors and associate it with a specific event.

•	You can manually add a visitor to an event.

•	When necessary, you can remove a visitor by clicking on the "trash" icon.

•	Additionally, you have the ability to ban a visitor by clicking on the icon located next to the "trash" icon.
